My name is Tamara Nikolaevna Rusinova.

I am a mathematics teacher with 30 years of experience.

Work Experience#

Now#

Since 2024, I have been working as a mathematics teacher at the State Budgetary Educational Institution of the City of Moscow “School No. 1557 named after Pyotr Leonidovich Kapitsa” (GBOU School No. 1557 named after P.L. Kapitsa).

In this academic year:

  • Mathematics for the Interested in $5^а, 5^в, 5^м$;
  • Mathematics for Enthusiasts in $6^а, 6^б, 6^в$;
  • Algebra, Geometry, Probability and Statistics in $7^б, 8^б, 8^в$.

Previously#

From 2015 to 2024, I worked as a mathematics and computer science teacher, Deputy Director for Curriculum and Instruction, and Principal at the Mendeleevo Secondary School in the urban-type settlement of Mendeleevo, Solnechnogorsk District, Moscow Region (MBOU Mendeleevskaya Secondary School).

From 1996 to 2015, I worked as a mathematics and computer science teacher and Deputy Director for Curriculum and Instruction at the Lesnoy Secondary School in the settlement of Lesnoy, Verkhnekamsky District, Kirov Region (MKOU Secondary School in Lesnoy).
